2010

The passing of Andy Irons

Top: Andy Irons. Bottom: Andy Irons - I Surf because short film

A big shock to the surfing world. Andy Irons passes away at the age of 32 while still being in the prime of his career. Andy being Kelly Slater's biggest rival stole 3 world titles off him. The Governor of Hawaii declared February 13 "Andy Irons day".
