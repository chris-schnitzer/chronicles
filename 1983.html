Tom Curren

Curren at J-Bay

A legend of the sport named Tom Curren was introduced to the world of pro surfing in 1983. His style, grace and maturity of his surfing which are well beyond the years of many of his older competitors who he startled from the get go. Curren won 3 world titles in 1985, 1986 and 1990 with 33 world tour victories throughout his illustrious career. He retired in the early nineties.
